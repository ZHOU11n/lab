BRIEF INTRODUCTION
======
Prof. Weijun Zhou: Chinese, male, born in December 1962 in Ningbo city (China), married and one adult son.
Prof. Zhou has released 8 new rapeseed (Canola) varieties, obtained over 20 inventive patents, and published more than 200 refereed papers, 20 books / chapters and 80 conference and technical articles.  Dr. Zhou was awarded over 10 scientific research prizes and honors.  In his postdoctoral and postgraduate supervision, 18 Post-doctoral fellows, 38 PhD students and 47 Masters from various countries have been awarded (currently 10 Post-docs, 8 PhD and 5 Master students).
Dr. Zhou is the Executive Member of Crop Science Society of China, the Executive Member of Oil Crops Committee of Crop Science Society of China, the Executive Member of Weed Science Society of China, the Deputy Chair of Crop Physiology Committee of Crop Science Society of China, the Executive Member of Rapeseed Breeding Consulting Committee, the Executive Member of Rapeseed Agronomy and Industry Consulting Committee, and the Executive Member of Crop Science Society of Zhejiang Province, China.
Prof. Zhou spent 6 years of study and research at universities and institutes in countries like UK, Sweden, Germany, Japan and the USA.  Dr. Zhou was invited to serve in international / scientific organizing committee and journals board members, to chair or co-chair symposium / workshop or sessions (such as invited to chair the Agronomy and Production section of 12th International Rapeseed Congress, the Coordinator of 6th International Weed Science Congress), and to review grant proposals, books / chapters, professorship / doctoral dissertations and manuscripts for over 40 academic journals. Also invited as the Guest Editor for the journal “Pesticide Biochemistry and Physiology” and “Plant Nano Biology”.


QUALIFICATIONS
======
* 1994.09–1998.07: Ph.D, Crop Physiological Ecology, Zhejiang Agricultural University, Hangzhou, China
* 1987.09–1988.09:  M.Sc, Plant Genetic Resources, University of Birmingham, Birmingham, UK
* 1979.11–1983.07:  B.Sc, Agronomy, Zhejiang Agricultural University, Hangzhou, China


RESEARCH INTERESTS
======
* Germplasm enhancement, biotechnology and breeding especially in oilseed crops including rapeseed (Canola);
* Crop physiology and ecology, chemical and molecular regulation including parasitic plants; Weed biology and control;
* Sustainable crop production systems for quality and safety, and industry development.
